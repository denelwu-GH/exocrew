# Cross-functional role matrix

| Role | Core questions | Required output |
|---|---|---|
| Product | Who benefits, what changes, what is out, what can fail? | Value, scope, workflow, boundaries, acceptance |
| Engineering | Where does truth live, what contracts change, how is failure handled? | Architecture boundary, implementation plan, migration impact |
| Test | Which risk must be disproved, at what evidence level? | Risk matrix, targeted checks, evidence report |
| Operations | What can write, deploy, break, roll back, or require recovery? | Change package, approval, rollback, post-verification |

The delivery lead owns sequencing and closure. Role outputs advise the decision; they do not expand authorization.
